Frequently asked question

Still in doubt? Please do not hesitate to reach out to us on our Contact Us form.

Must I be a registed user to start donating?

Yes, this is to ensure that all reward points are accurately recorded.

What items can I donate?

Some items we accept include used clothing, furniture, stationery, electrical appliances, and non-perishable food. All items should also be in usable condition so that we are able to give these items a second life. You can find the ful list of items here.

How many items can I donate in one pick-up schedule?

To ensure that items are being transported safely, we are limiting each pick-up to only three items.

Are there any expiry dates to my reward points?

No, however the redeemable reward items changes quite frequently, so we suggest that you redeem your desired rewards at the soonest!

What are the operating hours of the pick-up services?

Our pick-up courier services are available from 11am to 6pm daily. You may schedule a booking here.

Where does the items that I donate go to?

We partner with many charitable organizations that help support children, elderlies, and also animals. You may indicate your preferred beneficiary if applicable, else we will help route your items to organizations that might need certain items more. You may view the full list of beneficiaries here.
